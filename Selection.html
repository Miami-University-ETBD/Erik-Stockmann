This page is a work in progress

This site will eventually be built as an online Clothing Brand Store known as Requiem.

Requiem's goal is to create cool clothing designs, with non-toxic materials and dyes.

The clothing Industry sadly has an overemphasis on using harsh chemicals and toxic materials in dyes. There are several sites that sell organic clothing, branded with nature or eco-styled designs that don't always look cool or hype. So the founders of Requiem thought why not attempt to create clothing that is kind to your body, and cool and exciting to wear.
