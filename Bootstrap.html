Sculpture
Scultpure involves creating art in a 3-d form using a kind of material. plants, gods, humans, and animals are common figures for sculpting. Clay, metal, and marble are common materials. The ancient Greek world was dominated by the sculpture, especially using marble. The ancient Greeks created statues with astounding depth and detail.
Renaissance
in the 1400s, starting in Italy, the Renaissance created an explosion of art in Europe. There was an increased interest in the world of the classical greek and romans. Many of the subject nmatter came from the antique. Many styles of vivid oil painting were discovered with never before seen detail in paintings.
Woodcarving
Woodcarving is a technique humans have used since the dawn of time. For much of history, it has been done using a sharp object such as a knife or tool to carve away at a section of wood to create a design. It has been used to decorate buildings, ships, weapons, etc. Germany in particular is well known for wood carving. More recently there have been techniques developed to use a laser to cut wood. This technique is called lasercutting.
